## 📖 Abstract

Secure Contract Book is a Python-based digital contract protection and verification project developed to provide safe storage, structured monitoring, and tamper-resistant management of sensitive contractual records.

Manual contract handling and unprotected digital storage often lead to:

- unauthorized modifications,
- accidental record loss,
- insecure accessibility,
- contract fraud,
- poor verification transparency.

This system introduces a secure software workflow where agreement records are digitally maintained, verified, and monitored through controlled Python logic.

The project can be used for:

- legal record management,
- secure agreement tracking,
- business contract verification,
- digital document integrity protection,
- academic security system demonstration.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Important legal and business contracts require:

- safe storage,
- secure access,
- verification authenticity,
- protected record maintenance,
- tamper-resistant handling.

Traditional manual record books or unsecured files do not provide reliable contract integrity.

Therefore, a secure computerized contract verification system is needed.
